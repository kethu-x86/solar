5kW On-Grid Solar Setup Cost in Kerala

Your guide to understanding solar investment with government subsidies

Executive Summary

A 5kW on-grid solar system in Kerala is a smart investment, offering significant electricity bill reductions and environmental benefits. The estimated upfront cost, before subsidies, ranges from ₹3,15,000 to ₹3,65,000. With the Central Financial Assistance (CFA) of ₹78,000 under the PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana, the effective net cost is reduced to an estimated range of ₹2,37,000 to ₹2,87,000.

This system is projected to generate substantial bimonthly savings of ₹9,200 to ₹10,200 on electricity bills, leading to a rapid payback period of typically 4 to 8 years. After this, electricity generated is virtually free for the system's lifespan, which commonly exceeds 25 years.

Financial Benefits and Return on Investment

A 5kW solar power plant can generate approximately 20-25 units of electricity per day, leading to significant monthly and bimonthly savings.

Ongrid Solar Capacity Monthly Electricity Generation (units) Estimated Monthly Savings (INR) Estimated Bimonthly Savings (INR)
5 kWp 600-900 ₹3,500 – ₹5,000 ₹9,200 – ₹10,200

The investment typically yields a rapid payback period of 4 to 8 years. After this, electricity generated is essentially free for the lifespan of the panels (25+ years).

Net metering allows you to feed excess electricity back into the grid, earning credits. The current settlement rate for net energy banked is ₹3.15 per unit.
